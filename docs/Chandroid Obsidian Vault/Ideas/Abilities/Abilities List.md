## Chandroid abilities

+ [[Shoulder Upgrade]] Allows [[Chandroid]] to lift heavy objects.

+ [[Bouncy Skirt]] - bounce player up to higher platforms/bounce objects in motion
+ [[Tablet]] - allows player to see and command through [[Chandroid]]'s eyes(experimental tech, not on market yet) (waterproof)
+ [[Grapple Hitch]] - allows player to pull [[Chandroid]] to him. Looks like a trailer hitch on the small of her back. She is held in his arms, he can barely walk until put down
+ [[Codecs]] (special glasses) to decrypt textual hints for optional finds (involved in secret ending)

+ [[Laser Pointer]] - point at prisms to light world & activate prism puzzles

+ [[Chainsaw]] - break any objects made of wood (context independent)

+ [[Oven Mitts]] - grasp high voltage objects

+ [[Hair Net]] - prevents hair from getting tangled in spider webs, allowing her to pass through

**Whenever [[Chandroid]] gets a new ability, small 50's style B/W commercial plays showing the ability in Fallout style comedic fashion**


## Player abilities

+ [[Rewire]] - gives scanner the ability to rewire certain puzzle elements
+ [[Sprocket Gun]] - allows player to launch away from where they're looking
+ [[Grapple]] - allows player to swing from grapple points, cross horizontal gaps
+ [[Inspector Specs]] (scan glasses) - allows player to gather essential and non-essential information from the environment
+ [[Metal Boots]] - negates fall damage
+ [[Floaties]] - allows player to swim
+ [[Pry Tool]] crowbar-like tool - pry open doors/panels
