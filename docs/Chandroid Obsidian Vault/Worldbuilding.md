#### slang
words or phrases that condense down concepts to repeatable memes, used by subcultures to establish themselves as different, particularly the youth
concepts like actions, values, virtues
they take the form of concretes, not abstractions
metaphors
Kids of today are most interested in electronics, so their slang is centered around computer and robotics terminology
- chipped - joyful, excited
- yellow - cringe, pathetic, weak
- screen/pixel - poser, one worried about others' opinions, performative
- fan/exhaust fan, fanning - someone talking bullshit
- exhaust/fog - said bullshit, lies, falsehoods
- oiled - good, fast, sleek, cool

#### older generation's slang
focused more on economic terms, mechanics, industry
- gassed up - energized
- steam powered - highly driven, internally motivated


Generational Divide
While the older generation were the industrialists, the entrepreneurs, the builders, the foundation layers, the new generation are the computer kids, the tech enthusiasts, the robotics nerds. They carry similar ideals, and have a fond love for the history their parents built, the world they grew up in, but they also define themselves a little differently, chase after new advancements, new frontiers.

The even further previous generation were the explorers, the merchants, the traders.


The suburbs is the major area where you see the older ways of life, the parental generation. Their architecture is of a Streamline Moderne style, their cars are more reflective chrome and brass. They are more likely to walk dogs, own non-sentient robots, and take care of their lawn.
