# GOALS
Require the player to have the [[Quantum Traveling Device]] before starting to learn about lifts

Teach the player about the lift mechanic

Lead the player to discovering and entering the [[Maid Factory]] through the roof.

Force the player to learn that they can use [[Chandroid]]'s positioning for timing upon trying to exit the area

# ELEMENTS
Lift
Commands
Pad
Doors

# QUESTIONS
How to prevent player from moving on without [[Chandroid]] after getting up?

>Use wall with locked door and [[Chandroid Terminal]] to prevent progression

How do vehicles get in and out of the parking area for [[Maid Factory]]?
>Ramp? Tunnel?

# IDEAS
Use the [[Lift]] plenty to get player used to how it works

# SEQUENCE
Player enters the room to see a locked gate with a lift visible behind it.
Player must enter the battery and solve its puzzle to restore power to the gate.
Once power is restored, player can open gate and proceed
Player is presented with lift and pad placed in easily useable distance from each other
Must get himself and [[Chandroid]] up to higher spot to proceed
[[Chandroid]] is required to open nearby door using [[Chandroid Terminal]].
Player finds a spot to drop down, with [[Lift]] at bottom which can lower [[Chandroid]] by activating it from either the top or the bottom (this puzzle is more difficult coming back than it is entering)
Walking forward, player spies [[Maid Factory]].
Must use lift to enter, but pad for activation is slightly hidden/further away
Player enters [[Maid Factory]]
