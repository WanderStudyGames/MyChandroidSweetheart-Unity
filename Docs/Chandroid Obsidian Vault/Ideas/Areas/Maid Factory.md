# GOALS/CONSTRAINTS
Player fell into [[Maid Factory]] from above, no way out except to open the front garage door.

No way to open garage door without restoring power via battery

Have player aquire [[Rewire]] and use it to open an exit
Plenty of practice with the [[Rewire]].

Have story beat with [[Abandoned Maid Bot]] in which you help her, allowing her to help you

# ELEMENTS
Pad
Lift
Door
Rewire(after collect)
Darkness(scary)

# GAMEPLAY BEATS
Funnel player into manager's room after allowing them to explore a bit

Player finds closed garage door, clearly marked as the exit, before finding manager's room and battery.

# QUESTIONS
Why have [[Abandoned Maid Bot]]? What makes her compelling?
>She's cute and adds more character to the world. Gives the player a sense of satisfaction for helping her.

Isn't it a bit cruel for the manufacturers to leave a maid bot in a helpless state after moving buildings?
>They're not done moving everything out, clearly. They would have been back eventually, but now you get to relieve a maid bot's suffering.

Is feeling around in the dark going to be fun? Will there be sufficient interest to warrant some basic in-the-dark puzzles? Or will it be frustrating to players?

If this is a puzzle game, wouldn't darkness just be a massive unnecessary source of noise for the puzzle? If so, would the noise be worth it for the experience?
>How about, only 1st room in the facility is in total darkness, and everything else has some weak form of light around.

Is [[Abandoned Maid Bot]] optional? Perhaps with a cosmetic reward for rescuing her?
>Perhaps cosmetic is found when returning to this facility with a later upgrade, like [[Pry Tool]]

# SEQUENCE
- Player drops into dark room, almost pitch black except for one visible light at the end of the room. A loud echoed crash is heard as the player lands.
- Sudden spike in scariness after a bright outside
- Player finds their way into a dimly-lit room.
- Robot parts (half-severed bodies, limbs, heads) strewn about on the floor and tables look like something out of Outlast. (Scans indicate this must be the remnants after a quick move-out, and that the movers are likely not finished with the job)
- In the next room, player discovers [[Abandoned Maid Bot]], who is quietly sobbing due to being incapacitated (or she's switched off and sitting prominently in the room, such that it's obvious she can be interacted with somehow)
- Player happens upon the front garage entrance, with glowing red "Insufficient Power" warning light
- Player drops into (manager's office? boiler room?), in which they'll find a battery attached to the circuit breaker
- Player restores power, allowing them to access the [[Rewire]] module for the [[Inspector Specs]]
- Player must complete a series of easy rewire puzzles to get back to garage door/[[Abandoned Maid Bot]]
- Player connects up rewire to release/activate [[Abandoned Maid Bot]]
- She is so relieved and thankful that she opens the garage door, allowing the player to leave.
- Player leaves through garage door to get back outside.
