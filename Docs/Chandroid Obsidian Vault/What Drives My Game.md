When the player is doing the initial exploring, the world design is the main driver. 

Then, they'll meet characters which will give them localized problems to solve. Solving those problems involves either gathering collectibles, or solving puzzles.

Solving enough local problems may net the player a key item or upgrade which will allow them to continue exploring and find a boss area, containing puzzles/enemies.
