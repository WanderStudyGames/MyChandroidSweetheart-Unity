<color=yellow>Doctor Henry Nulove</color>
Founder of NuLove Companion Clinic, third party Chandroid assembly.
Doctor NuLove went to college for computer science and engineering. 
Initially pursuing a job at HoverTek, NuLove found little success. Thus, he turned his efforts to helping the common man. 
After a number of failed business ventures, NuLove was ready to give up.
But he noticed a discrepancy in the Chandroid market. Buyers were either stuck with proprietary Chandroids from the major manufacturers, or left to figure out how to assemble one themselves. 
This became the driving motivation for Nulove's Companion Clinic.
"Love is simple, love is personal" was their slogan, and it stuck.
