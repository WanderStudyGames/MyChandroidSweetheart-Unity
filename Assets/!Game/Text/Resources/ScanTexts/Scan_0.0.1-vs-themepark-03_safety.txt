KookyLand Staff Note
"The health board has reached out to us about potential safety concerns regarding our Topple Town attraction. 
They've stated that it's possible for smaller children to be hurt or suffocated by falling towers of blocks. 
But we've sent a reply showing the precautions we've taken against this. 
Firstly, the cubes are made of a light foam, and weigh as much as 1lb maximum. 
Secondly, only children aged 13 and older are allowed to enter Topple Town without a parent's supervision.
So while we appreciate the concerns being shown, we've got it covered."
